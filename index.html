Toowek, a portable biometric attendance device designed especially for educators, enabling flexible, secure, and digital attendance management beyond fixed classroom infrastructure.

Why This device?

Traditional attendance methods suffer from:
  • Manual paper registers (time-consuming, error-prone)
  • Fixed biometric systems limited to one classroom
  • RFID-based systems vulnerable to proxy attendance
  • Poor organisation of subject-wise attendace data
Educator need a portable, secure, and subject-centric solution.

Solution overview

Toowek is a portable biometric attendance system that allows teachers to:
  • Carry the device to any classroom
  • Manage attendance independently of fixed infrastructure
  • Maintain subject-wise attendance records in a single device
It modernized attendance handling for digital classrooms.

Key Features

  • Portable design- Easy to carry and use in classroom
  • Fingerprint-Based Authentication- Prevents proxy and duplicate attendance
  • subect-Wise Attendance Management- Separate files for each subject
  • Centralized Record Storage- All attendance data in one device
  • Secure & Reliable- Biometric verification ensures authenticity

Technology & Components

  • Microcontroller: ESP32
  • Biometric Module: Fingerprint Sensor
  • Diplay: TFT Display
  • Authentication Method: Biometric (Fingerprint)
